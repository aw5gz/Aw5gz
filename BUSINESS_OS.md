- Messaging: Twilio + email provider
- AI layer: GPT agents for messaging, triage, reporting

### Workflow logic

2. AI intent classification (hot/warm/cold)
3. Hot lead: instant SMS + booking link + alert rep
4. Warm lead: nurture sequence + value assets
5. No response: cadence across SMS/email/voicemail drops
6. Booked call: reminders at 24h/3h/15m
7. Missed call: instant reschedule sequence
8. Closed-lost: reactivation trigger at day 30/60/90

- If lead-to-booking < 15% for 2 weeks → conversion audit
